
# Bank Customer Churn Analytics

## Overview
This project analyzes customer churn patterns in a retail banking business using SQL, Python, and Power BI.

The goal was to identify which customers are more likely to leave and understand the business factors behind churn.

This project was intentionally designed to look like a realistic fresher-level analytics project.

---

## Business Questions
- Which customers are leaving the bank?
- Which geography has the highest churn?
- Does balance affect churn?
- Which age groups are at risk?
- How does customer activity affect retention?

## Dataset Information
Dataset Source: Kaggle Customer Churn Dataset

Dataset Includes:
- Customer demographics
- Credit score
- Geography
- Balance
- Estimated salary
- Product usage
- Churn status

---

## Key Insights
- Germany showed higher churn compared to other regions.
- Older customers had relatively higher churn probability.
- Inactive members were more likely to leave.
- Customers with fewer products showed lower retention.
